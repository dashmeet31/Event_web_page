About the Event

PIETNextGEN was a dynamic and power-packed technical event organized by the Department of Computer Applications powered by UNSTOP the MET Club. The event aimed to provide a platform for students to showcase their creativity, innovation, and technical skills through impactful projects and solutions.

Students enthusiastically presented their brilliant and groundbreaking innovations, demonstrating real-world problem-solving through interactive models, applications, and live demos. The event was a celebration of ideas, where technology met creativity.

PIET NextGen served as a great opportunity for students to not only demonstrate their talents, but also to collaborate, build confidence, and engage with the spirit of innovation. It truly showcased the potential of the next generation of tech leaders.
